Bangaliana Multicultural Christchurch Community

Celebrating Culture, Unity, and Tradition in Christchurch

Bangaliana Multicultural Christchurch Community is a vibrant, inclusive organization dedicated to preserving and celebrating Bengali culture in the heart of New Zealand. Our community brings together individuals and families who share a love for Bengali heritage, fostering a sense of belonging and cultural pride.

Our Mission

  • Celebrate Bengali traditions and festivals with grandeur.
  • Create a welcoming platform for cultural exchange.
  • Promote social harmony through multicultural events.
  • Pass down Bengali heritage to future generations in Christchurch.

Durga Puja: Our Grand Celebration

The Durga Puja festival is the highlight of our calendar, where we come together to honor Goddess Durga with devotion, music, dance, and traditional rituals. This annual event showcases Bengali culture through:

  • 🎭 Cultural performances – Music, dance, and drama by local and international artists.
  • 🍛 Authentic Bengali cuisine – A feast of delicious homemade dishes.
  • 🙏 Puja rituals – Traditional prayers and offerings to Goddess Durga.
  • 🎉 Community engagement – Fun activities for families, kids, and seniors.
